## THE PROBLEM (I lived this everyday)

**Personal finance tracking sucks.** 

I've tried every app - Mint, YNAB, PocketGuard, you name it. They all have the same fatal flaw: *manual entry*. Who has time to categorize every ₹50 coffee or remember to log that Swiggy order at 11 PM?

The result? 90% of people abandon these apps within 3 months. We're stuck with:
- Bank statements that are cryptic ("TXN REF 847392847")  
- Multiple payment apps with zero consolidated view
- No insights into spending patterns that actually matter
- Zero understanding of where our money actually goes

**We have more data about our finances than ever before, but less understanding.**

---

## THE SOLUTION (That actually works)

**Slash reads your emails and automatically tracks everything.**

No manual entry. No scanning receipts. No remembering to categorize. Just connect Gmail and get complete financial visibility.

### How it works:
1. **Secure Gmail Integration** → OAuth access to financial emails only
2. **AI-Powered Extraction** → Parse transaction amounts, merchants, categories from 100+ sources
3. **Smart Analytics** → Spending insights, behavioral patterns, trends over time  
4. **Natural Language Queries** → "How much did I spend on food this month?"

**Current Focus:** Starting with Swiggy analytics (food delivery is the #1 expense category for our demographic). Users see immediate value, then we expand to cover their entire financial life.
